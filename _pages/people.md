### Dr. Derek D. Lichti

**Professor, Department of Geomatics Engineering**
Schulich School of Engineering, University of Calgary

Derek Lichti is a Professor of Geomatics Engineering at the University of
Calgary, where he has been a faculty member since 2008. He served as
Department Head from 2013 to 2018. He is the Editor-in-Chief of the
*ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing* and a recipient of
the Schwidefsky Medal from ISPRS and the University of Calgary's Peak
Scholar award.

His research focuses on imaging metrology — precision 3D reality capture
from terrestrial laser scanners, digital cameras, range cameras, and
biomedical imaging systems. He holds a PhD and MSc from the University of
Calgary and a BTech from Ryerson University.
